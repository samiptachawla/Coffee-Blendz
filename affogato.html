Affogato Recipe

Origin and Details

Affogato is an Italian dessert that combines espresso with a scoop of vanilla gelato or ice cream. The word "affogato" means "drowned" in Italian, referring to the act of pouring hot espresso over the cold ice cream.

Recipe

To make an Affogato:

  1. Place a scoop of vanilla gelato or ice cream in a serving glass.
  2. Brew a shot of espresso using your espresso machine.
  3. Pour the hot espresso over the gelato or ice cream.
  4. Optionally, garnish with chocolate shavings or a sprinkle of cocoa powder.
  5. Serve immediately and enjoy your delicious Affogato!
