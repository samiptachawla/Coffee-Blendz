Cappuccino Recipe

Origin and Details

Cappuccino is a traditional Italian coffee drink made with equal parts of espresso, steamed milk, and milk foam. It originated in Italy and is known for its creamy texture and balanced flavor profile.

Recipe

To make a Cappuccino:

  1. Brew a shot of espresso using your espresso machine.
  2. Steam milk to create a velvety microfoam texture.
  3. Pour the steamed milk over the espresso shot, holding back the foam with a spoon.
  4. Spoon a dollop of milk foam on top of the milk.
  5. Optionally, dust with cocoa powder or cinnamon for added flavor.
  6. Enjoy your delicious Cappuccino!
