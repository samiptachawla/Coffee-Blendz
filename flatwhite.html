Flat White Recipe

Origin and Details

Flat White is a popular coffee beverage that originated in Australia or New Zealand, although there is debate about its exact origins. It is similar to a latte but with a higher ratio of coffee to milk and a velvety microfoam texture.

Recipe

To make a Flat White:

  1. Brew a double shot of espresso using your espresso machine.
  2. Steam milk to create a velvety microfoam texture.
  3. Pour the steamed milk over the espresso shots, creating a white dot on the surface.
  4. Swirl the milk and espresso together to combine.
  5. Enjoy your delicious Flat White!
