Macchiato Recipe

Origin and Details

Macchiato, short for caffe macchiato, is a coffee beverage that originated in Italy. It is made by adding a small amount of steamed milk or milk foam to a shot of espresso, creating a layered and bold-flavored drink.

Recipe

To make a Macchiato:

  1. Brew a shot of espresso using your espresso machine.
  2. Add a small dollop of steamed milk or milk foam to the espresso shot.
  3. Optionally, drizzle with caramel or flavored syrup for added sweetness.
  4. Enjoy your delicious Macchiato!
