Mocha Recipe

Origin and Details

Mocha, short for caffe mocha, is a popular coffee beverage that combines espresso, chocolate, and steamed milk. It originated in Italy and is known for its rich and indulgent flavor profile.

Recipe

To make a Mocha:

  1. Brew a shot of espresso using your espresso machine.
  2. Heat chocolate syrup or cocoa powder with milk until warm and well mixed.
  3. Pour the chocolate mixture over the espresso shot.
  4. Optional: Top with whipped cream and a sprinkle of cocoa powder or chocolate shavings.
  5. Enjoy your delicious Mocha!
