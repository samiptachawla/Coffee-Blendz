Latte Recipe

Origin and Details

Latte, short for caffe latte, is a popular coffee beverage that originated in Italy. It is made with espresso and steamed milk, topped with a small amount of milk foam. Latte is known for its creamy texture and smooth flavor.

Recipe

To make a Latte:

  1. Brew a shot of espresso using your espresso machine.
  2. Steam milk to create a creamy texture and froth.
  3. Pour the steamed milk over the espresso shot, holding back the foam with a spoon.
  4. Top the latte with a dollop of milk foam.
  5. Optional: Add flavorings such as vanilla or caramel syrup to customize your latte.
  6. Enjoy your delicious Latte!
