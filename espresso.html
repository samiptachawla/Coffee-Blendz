Espresso Recipe

Origin and Details

Espresso is a concentrated coffee beverage brewed by forcing hot water under pressure through finely-ground coffee beans. It originated in Italy and is the base for many other coffee drinks.

Recipe

To make an espresso:

  1. Grind 18-20 grams of coffee beans to a fine consistency.
  2. Preheat your espresso machine and portafilter.
  3. Distribute the coffee grounds evenly in the portafilter and tamp firmly.
  4. Lock the portafilter into the machine and start the extraction process.
  5. Extract the espresso for approximately 25-30 seconds, resulting in a shot of rich, concentrated coffee.
