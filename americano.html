Americano Recipe

Origin and Details

Americano is a popular coffee drink believed to have originated during World War II when American soldiers in Europe diluted espresso with hot water to mimic the taste of drip coffee back home. It is characterized by its smooth flavor and strength.

Recipe

To make an Americano:

  1. Brew a shot of espresso using your espresso machine.
  2. Heat water in a separate vessel until it reaches near-boiling temperature.
  3. Pour hot water over the espresso shot, filling the cup to your desired strength.
  4. Adjust the ratio of espresso to hot water according to your preference.
  5. Enjoy your freshly brewed Americano!
